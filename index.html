Bachelor of Philosophy (Honours Diploma, Universidad de Buenos Aires). I coursed a Master Degree on Historic Reasearch at Universidad de San Andrés and now I am finishing my PhD thesis on Philosophy with a doctoral grant by Consejo Nacional de Investigaciones Científicas y Técnicas (CONICET).

I am a member of the research teams of the Centro de Documentación e Investigación de la Cultura de Izquierda (CeDInCI), where I am member of the Academic Committee of the institution, of the Organizing Committee of scientific events and of the Advisory Committee of the digital magazines portal américalee.cedinci.org.

My research topics are concerned with the reception theory of philosophical currents, links between politics and philosophy in Argentine history, and the theoretical developments of left thought in the country. To date, I have published ten articles in specialized journals in Argentina (5), Spain (2), Venezuela (1), Chile (1) and Colombia (1). In addition, I have published a book on documentary sources of anarchism in Argentina (Buenos Aires: Anarres-CeDInCI, 2017) and a compilation of texts by the young Carlos Astrada with Natalia Bustelo (Buenos Aires: Las Cuarenta, 2018).

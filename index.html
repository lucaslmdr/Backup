I finished my PhD thesis on Philosophy (University of Buenos Aires-UBA) with a doctoral scholarship by the National Council of Scientific and Technical Research (CONICET). I coursed a Master Degree on Historic Research at Universidad de San Andrés (UDESA). And I am Bachelor of Philosophy (Honours Diploma, University of Buenos Aires).

I am a researcher of the Centro de Documentación e Investigación de la Cultura de Izquierda (CeDInCI-UNSAM), where I am a member of the Academic Committee of the institution, of the Organizing Committee of scientific events and member of the Advisory Committee of the digital magazines portal americalee.cedinci.org. In addition, I am a member of the Editorial Committee of the journal Politicas de la memoria (ISSN 1668-4885).
My research topics are concerned with the reception theory of philosophical currents, links between politics and philosophy in Argentine history and the theoretical developments of left thought. To date, I have published ten articles in specialized journals in Argentina (6), Spain (1), Venezuela (1), Chile (1), Holland,(1) and Colombia (1); and five arcticles in not peer-reviewed journals. In addition, I have published a book on documentary sources of anarchism in Argentina (Anarres-CeDInCI, 2018) and a compilation of texts by the young Carlos Astrada with Natalia Bustelo (Las Cuarenta, 2019).
